The flag

The national flag of Lebanon features a horizontal triband design of two colors. These colors are meant to represent purity, peace, and blood shed of the nation. However, the flag also prominently features a cedar tree directly in the center.




The red bands on the flag are meant to symbolize the blood that was shed by the Lebanese in defending the nation against enemies.







The white color is used to represent snow, which symbolizes peace and purity.
